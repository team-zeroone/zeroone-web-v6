### Fluent by Design
Language learning often stalls at the friction of rote memorization. Lexigram eliminates this barrier by weaving French vocabulary acquisition into a dynamic, game-like ecosystem. By merging sleek interface design in Figma with a high-performance Flutter architecture, we created an intuitive environment where users move seamlessly between puzzles, flashcards, and interactive assessments.

### Engineered for Retention
The engine driving Lexigram is as robust as it is invisible. We leveraged FastAPI and Kafka to manage real-time progress tracking and spaced-repetition logic, ensuring that content adapts to the learner at the precise moment of need. This architecture allows the app to process complex user behavior data without latency, maintaining a fluid experience that keeps engagement high. 

Beyond mere functionality, Lexigram is built for consistent mastery. By integrating structured progress analytics with reactive, bite-sized challenges, the app moves students from casual practice to genuine, real-world linguistic confidence. It is a modern solution for those who demand more from their educational tools: a marriage of sophisticated back-end engineering and human-centered design that turns the challenge of a new language into a repeatable, rewarding habit.
