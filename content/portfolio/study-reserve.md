# Study Reserve

StudyReserve stands as a pioneering cloud-based education platform, empowering educational institutes and individual lecturers to seamlessly host tutoring sessions and manage diverse course content. For over five years, ZeroOne Technologies has partnered strategically with the StudyReserve team, delivering robust and high-quality software that has propelled the platform's continuous evolution.

What began as a core content management system has matured into a comprehensive digital learning ecosystem. StudyReserve now features a polished public-facing landing site, a full product experience, and sophisticated dedicated dashboards for students, teachers, and administrators. ZeroOne's expertise facilitated the development of native, cross-platform student applications for iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows, ensuring ubiquitous access. A cornerstone of the platform is its cutting-edge secure content delivery, incorporating advanced restrictions against screenshots, screen recording, and casting—safeguarding intellectual property. The entire solution is meticulously engineered as one continuous, seamlessly integrated system, offering unparalleled reliability and a unified user experience across all touchpoints.
