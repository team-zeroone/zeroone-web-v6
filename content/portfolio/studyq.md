### Elevating Academic Preparation
StudyQ transforms the traditional study cycle into a dynamic, tech-driven experience. Built with a robust stack comprising Flutter for mobile agility and a Next.js web interface, the platform serves as a bridge between subject matter experts and learners. By prioritizing intuitive content delivery, we have removed the friction typically associated with exam preparation, allowing educators to deploy curated, high-impact question sets with ease.

### Architecture Built for Scale
Our backend architecture leverages the power of Nest.js and Postgres to manage complex data relationships, while Supabase and Firebase ensure real-time synchronization across all devices. Hosted on DigitalOcean, StudyQ maintains high availability and low latency, ensuring that learning remains uninterrupted regardless of scale. The platform is designed for deep revision and concept reinforcement, offering students a clean, focused interface to engage with diverse materials. By fostering a ecosystem where high-quality content is easily accessible, StudyQ enables a more efficient approach to knowledge acquisition. This is not just a digital workbook, but a scalable infrastructure designed to evolve alongside the modern student.
